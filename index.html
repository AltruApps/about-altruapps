What is AltruApps

AltruApps refers to the development organization aiming to democratize applications meeting these attributes:

  • Ethically positive impact on their user
  • Hard to find free and ad-free versions of
  • Has a single, well-defined purpose

AltruApps pledges to keep developed as following these promises:

  1. No advertisements
  2. No subscriptions or fees except possibly a one-time purchase to pay for development
  3. No selling of personal data
  4. No evil
